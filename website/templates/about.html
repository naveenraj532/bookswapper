The Book Swapper Solution

A member in a community might require a book where another member in the same community might have it and could be anonymous, and there could be a high possibility that the person could be an outsider. The idea is to develop a portal that can create interrelation between the community members and exchange information about the books they own. The community members should be able to list the books they are willing to exchange with others in the forum, and the database should store the information about the books listed by every user. Members should be able to search for the required book, and if it is available, the owner of the book can be contacted, private discussion can be done between them, and exchanges can be initiated.
